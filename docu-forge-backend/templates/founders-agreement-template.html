FOUNDER EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT



This Founder Employment Agreement ("Agreement") is made and entered into on {{ effective.date }}, by and between:

{{ company.name }}, a company incorporated under the laws of India, having its registered office at {{ company.address }} (hereinafter referred to as the “Company”, which expression shall, unless repugnant to the context or meaning thereof, include its successors and permitted assigns),

AND

{{ founder.name }}, residing at {{ founder.address }} (hereinafter referred to as the “Founder”, which expression shall, unless repugnant to the context or meaning thereof, include his/her heirs, executors, administrators, and permitted assigns).

WHEREAS:

A. The Company desires to employ the Founder in the capacity of {{ founder.designation }}, and the Founder has agreed to such employment under the terms and conditions set forth herein.
B. The Founder acknowledges that he/she will play a key role in the strategic and operational functions of the Company.
C. Both parties wish to define their respective rights and obligations during the course of such employment.

1. Appointment

1.1 The Company hereby appoints the Founder, and the Founder accepts such appointment, as {{ founder.designation }} of the Company with effect from {{ effective.date }}.

1.2 The Founder shall perform all duties customarily associated with such position and any other responsibilities assigned by the Board of Directors of the Company.

2. Term

2.1 This Agreement shall commence on {{ effective.date }} and shall continue unless terminated earlier in accordance with the provisions of this Agreement.

2.2 The Founder’s employment shall be subject to satisfactory performance and compliance with Company policies.

3. Duties and Responsibilities

3.1 The Founder shall devote his/her full time, skill, and attention to the business of the Company.

3.2 The Founder agrees to perform such duties as are assigned by the Board and act in the best interests of the Company.

3.3 The Founder shall comply with all laws, Company rules, and policies applicable to his/her position.

3.4 The Founder shall not, without prior written approval, engage in any other business, profession, or employment.

4. Place of Work

4.1 The Founder shall perform his/her duties primarily at the Company’s office located at {{ company.address }}, or such other location as may be determined by the Company from time to time.

4.2 The Founder agrees to travel for business purposes as reasonably required.

5. Compensation and Benefits

5.1 The Founder shall receive a salary of ₹{{ founder.salary }}/- (Rupees {{ founder.salary.words }} only) per annum, payable in accordance with the Company’s standard payroll practices.

5.2 The Founder shall be entitled to participate in all benefit plans and programs generally made available to employees of similar standing.

5.3 The Company may, at its discretion, grant bonuses, incentives, or equity-based compensation to the Founder, as determined by the Board.

6. Expenses

6.1 The Company shall reimburse the Founder for all reasonable and necessary business expenses incurred in connection with the performance of duties, upon submission of proper documentation.

7. Confidentiality and Intellectual Property

7.1 The Founder acknowledges that, during the course of employment, he/she will have access to confidential and proprietary information of the Company.

7.2 The Founder shall not, during or after the term of employment, disclose or use any such information for any purpose other than the performance of his/her duties.

7.3 All inventions, discoveries, designs, or works developed by the Founder during the course of employment that relate to the business of the Company shall be the sole property of the Company.

7.4 The Founder agrees to execute any necessary documents to assign ownership rights of such intellectual property to the Company.

8. Non-Compete and Non-Solicitation

8.1 During the term of this Agreement and for a period of {{ noncompete.period }} months following termination, the Founder shall not engage in any business that competes with the Company.

8.2 The Founder shall not solicit or attempt to solicit any employee, consultant, or customer of the Company during the same period.

9. Termination

9.1 This Agreement may be terminated:
(a) By either party upon {{ notice.period }} days’ written notice.
(b) By the Company without notice for cause, including but not limited to breach of this Agreement, misconduct, or failure to perform duties.
(c) By the Company upon payment in lieu of notice.

9.2 Upon termination, the Founder shall immediately return all Company property, documents, and confidential materials.

10. Severance

10.1 If terminated without cause, the Founder shall be entitled to a severance payment equivalent to {{ severance.amount }} months of base salary.

10.2 No severance shall be payable in cases of termination for cause or voluntary resignation.

11. Conflict of Interest

11.1 The Founder shall promptly disclose to the Company any interest that may conflict with the performance of duties.

11.2 The Founder shall not use his/her position for personal gain other than compensation expressly approved by the Company.

12. Notices

12.1 Any notice under this Agreement shall be in writing and delivered personally, by courier, or by registered post to the following addresses:

To the Company:
{{company.name }}
{{ company.address }}

To the Founder:
{{ founder.name }}
{{ founder.address }}

12.2 Notices shall be deemed received when delivered personally or three (3) days after dispatch by registered post.

13. Governing Law and Jurisdiction

13.1 This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of India.

13.2 The courts at {{ jurisdiction.city }} shall have exclusive jurisdiction to adjudicate any disputes arising from this Agreement.

14. Entire Agreement

14.1 This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the parties with respect to the subject matter and supersedes all prior agreements or representations.

14.2 Any modification must be in writing and signed by both parties.

15. Severability

15.1 If any provision of this Agreement is held invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall remain in full force and effect.

16. Waiver

16.1 Failure of either party to enforce any provision of this Agreement shall not be construed as a waiver of any rights under it.

17. Assignment

17.1 The Founder shall not assign or transfer any rights or obligations under this Agreement without prior written consent of the Company.

17.2 The Company may assign this Agreement to any successor or affiliate.

18. Counterparts

18.1 This Agreement may be executed in counterparts, each of which shall be deemed an original but all of which together shall constitute one and the same instrument.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF

The parties hereto have executed this Founder Employment Agreement on the day and year first written above.
